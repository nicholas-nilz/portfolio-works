Hello, my name is Nicholas Leong, am a student currently studying in Republic Polytechnic in Digital Design & Development.


I've always had a passion for designing and in my free time, I like to cafe hop and listen to music. In group settings, I am known for my adaptability and ability to foster great team spirit. I believe in not allowing challenges to bring me down, but rather, I strive to find solutions to overcome them. I am seeking an internship position as a UI/UX designer to continue my passion for coding and designing. Through an internship, my goal is to absorb as much knowledge and experience as possible. How it all started..


My passion for art grew when I started to visit my friend's school exhibition. Her school would host yearly projects where students showcased their work for friends and families to visit. In addition, visiting museums became a hobby of mine. I would go to various museums to immerse myself in the world of art and culture. I found myself drawn deeper into the world of art, and it inspired me to pursue my studies in my current diploma. I hope to channel this passion and enthusiasm into my future and make a positive impact through my work.
