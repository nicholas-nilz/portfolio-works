Little Toy Kingdom

Little Toy Kingdom's website is a toy shop that caters to children of all ages. With a user-friendly interface and captivating design, the website offers a wide range of toys for babies, boys, and girls.

The website features a comprehensive selection of web pages, such as the Home page, which serves as a welcoming gateway to the world of Little Toy Kingdom. The Shop page offers a seamless shopping experience, allowing customers to browse and purchase their favorite toys effortlessly. The Babies, Boys, and Girls pages showcase a curated collection of toys specifically tailored to different age groups and gender preferences.

With its vibrant and child-friendly design, Little Toy Kingdom's website aims to inspire joy, foster imagination, and become the go-to online destination for parents and children alike where they will find little wonders that awaits for them.

Typography

Montserrat Regular & Semibold. I used Montserrat Regular for the contents and description of my pages, while Montserrat Semibold for the headings and sub-headings. I chose to use Montserrat as it provides clear and simple letters making it easy to read on digital screens. It also has a modern and minimalist feel which adds to the design and having the use of Montserrat Regular and Semibold provides a clear hierarchy as it helps to organise the information on a page to guide my reader's eyes through the difference level of importance.
