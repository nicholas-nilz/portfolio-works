Alphabet Fonts

The style of the alphabets that I chose to integrate my design with is bubble texts as I feel that bubble texts gives a lively and peaceful vibe which goes well with the overall pastel colour theme I chose for my design. This is because pastel colours are usually created from other colours, hence, they are very flexible and versatile in terms of going well with other colours to match the resulting colour scheme.

With pastels, one aspect of the colour scheme is that it brings a whole new depth and element to the colour that they derive from, thus, it makes it easy and pleasing for audience to bring their attention to the design as pastel colours lack the hues and saturation from normal saturated colours that are usually very striking. I chose to use analogous as it involves using three colours being next to one another and in this case, green, blues and indigo. These colours gives off a soft and soothing look to the overall design which I wanted as bubble letters are usually giving a playful and cheerful feeling to the work. Therefore, using the colours which are welcoming and subdued contrasts the lively characteristics of the design.

Working with complementary colours, they help to increase the look of the overall design as they not only pair well with one another, but it creates contrasts as one colour will be lighter than the other. In this case, the colours used were blues and browns as the blues stands out as the cool and light colour contrasts the dull brownish tones.
