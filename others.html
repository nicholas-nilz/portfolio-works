Aromatics

This was my work back in 2021 that I did for O levels. I chose the topic "Aromatics" as I have a passion to learn and differentiate scents of perfumes in my free time, hence, with this fascination with scents, I feel that in the current age, male confidence can come from his cologne. Thus, I used my art to show how I seek my identity and confidence from the fragrances I wear.

My final work canvas was in a shape of a perfume bottle, and since confidence is associated with the colour "Blue", I decided on a blue colour scheme moodboard as the background and to fill it with all of the things I like that are blue as well to show my identity as an individual.

The biggest portrait in the canvas is to show the confidence and masculinity when a male individual were to wear scents, hence, pairing it with the formal look of suit and makeup to enhance the meaning. The two smaller portraits at the top-right hand corner is to show how an individual is different when wearing scents and when they are not wearing. Therefore, the first smaller portrait is in a relaxed fit to show myself in my daily life, while the bottom portrait is more formal to represent that scents do make an individual more confident in themselves.
