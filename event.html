Event Poster

I designed this poster for an event promotion for DA:NS, I decided to go with a less visually striking background as I wanted my main focus to be on the dancers themselves, hence, I balanced it out by choosing a background with three spotlights that will bring my viewer’s attention straight to the dancers. Using the rule of thirds, I divided my work into the top section having all the information on the performance, the middleground having the attention to the dancers, and the foreground with the sponsors company. Since the middle spotlight shines the brightest, I played with scale to make the middle dancer the center of attention by scaling it bigger than the other two dancers, thus, grabbing the eyes of my viewers. Lastly, scaling my texts and making some larger than the rest such as the “Free Entry!” can immediately capture my viewers’ attention as people would be captivated by the word “Free” and would want to find out more about the upcoming performance.


Typography

Source Serif Variable & Myraid Pro. I chose to use these two fonts as it provides clear and simple letters making it easy to read on digital screens. It also has a modern and minimalist feel which adds to the design and having the use of Source Serif Variable & Myraid Pro provides a clear hierarchy as it helps to organise the information on a page to guide my reader's eyes through the difference level of importance.
