TOUCH Community

This mobile app was created to enhance the well-being, connectivity and independence of the elders. To engage them in different activities to upskill their knowledge and interests in current relevant topics. The app aims to provide a platform for the elders where they can explore and learn new skills, and expand their knowledge base. With the fun activities that are simple yet interesting to keep the senior citizens engaged in them, the app seeks to empower the elders to embrace a lifelong learning attitude.

To connect with the elders through ElderConnect, we aim for a simplicity, yet visually pleasing approach to design the app, as keeping the app easy to understand helps the elders to navigate around as most elders are not familiar with technology, hence, having instructions and simple recognisable icons is vital. In addition, making the app visually interesting helps enhance the overall visual appearance of the app and the user experience of it as well since factors such as typography, colour palettes etc. all comes in to place to work together to make the app nice.

All in all, we aim to engage them with a new idea and using our app also teaches them about current technology. Once they have tried our app, we hope to receive feedbacks from them on how they felt using the app, such as their experience, setbacks or any other remarks they might have for us to improve in future.

Typography

Lucida Sans Regular & DemiBold Roman. I used Lucida Sans Regular for the contents and description of my pages, while Lucida Sans DemiBold Roman for the headings and sub-headings. I chose to use Lucida Sans as it provides clear and simple letters making it easy to read on digital screens. It also has a modern and minimalist feel which adds to the design and having the use of Lucida Sans Regular & DemiBold Roman provides a clear hierarchy as it helps to organise the information on a page to guide my reader's eyes through the difference level of importance.
