SOI Learning Portal

The SOI Learning Portal is a dynamic and feature-rich website designed to enhance the user experience in online education. The portal offers a variety of courses, including PHP, React, iOS, Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and Java.

Key features include a dropdown menu for course selection, autocomplete functionality for the search box, and a notifications dropdown displaying both read and unread messages. The portal also incorporates a toggle feature indicating online/offline status, a tooltip for bookmarking cards, and a bookmark icon that adds selected cards to an Offcanvas body.

Additionally, the home page boasts a carousel feature for displaying course cards, ensuring a visually engaging experience for users. The design prioritizes responsiveness, adapting seamlessly to various screen sizes for optimal usability. As the SOI Learning Portal progresses, these front-end enhancements will contribute to a comprehensive and user-friendly educational platform.
