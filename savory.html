SavoryFlight

This app was created with the purpose to enhance the Singapore Airline travel experience by providing passengers with a personalized and diverse range of food options that cater to different dietary requirements, allergies, and preferences. The app aims to bridge the gap in the traditional airline meal selection by offering a broader and more inclusive range of food choices.

The target audience includes airline passengers who have dietary restrictions, allergies, or specific food preferences. This encompasses individuals with gluten intolerance, lactose intolerance, vegetarians, vegans, and other special dietary needs. In addition to that, the app caters to travelers looking for unique and diverse food options during their flights.

Some apps that are currently available in the market, “Airline Meal Allergy Tracker” and “In-flight Cuisine Guide”. The strengths of “Airline Meal Allergy Tracker” includes helping passenger track their allergies and dietary restrictions when ordering meals during flights. However, it lacks in broad range of food choices and options for travelers with various preferences.

The design gap SavoryFlight aims to bridge is the lack of diverse and inclusive food options for airline passengers, especially for Singapore Airline. It aims to go beyond the standard meal choices by providing a comprehensive selection that caters to different dietary restrictions, allergies, and preferences, ensuring an enjoyable and worry-free dining experiences for those traveling in and out of Singapore.

Features of SavoryFlight
Personalized Meal Selection - Passengers can input their dietary preferences and allergies to receive personalized meal suggestions for their flight.

Meal Reviews and Ratings - User can leave reviews and ratings for the meals they have tried, helping others make informed choices.

Special Requests - Passengers can make special requests for unique dietary needs, providing the airline with advance notice for meal preparation.

Allergy and Dietary Filters - Allows users to filter meal options based on specific allergies or dietary needs, such as gluten-free, lactose-free, vegetarian, etc.

Typography

Specter as the main font and Montserrat as the secondary font. The combination of these two fonts were chosen for their contrasting yet harmonious qualities. Specter offers a unique and distinctive appearance with its features, while Montserrat could provide clarity and readability as a secondary font. In this case, Specter is used for headings and larger text due to its bold and impactful design, which can definitely help establish hierarchy and grab attention. Montserrat, being more neutral, could then be used for body text, ensuring comfortable reading for our users.

In addition to the classy feels, the pairing of a modern, bold font like Specter with a classic and timeless font like Montserrat can create a balanced and contemporary design aesthetic. Furthermore, the fonts could work well across various devices and screen sizes due to their design characteristics.
