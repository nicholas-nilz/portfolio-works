Mobile App Portfolio

The portfolio created is to showcase the journey as a student studying Diploma in Digital Design and Development and to exhibit the works that a student would do when taking this diploma. This portfolio serves as not only a representation to display the work of a student, but also to reflect their passion and creativity in how they would showcase the skills and knowledge they learn in this diploma that they are taking.

The audience of this portfolio mainly includes potential employers, clients, fellow designers, or anyone who is interested in the design industry. This portfolio is made to capture the attention of these people to present them with a design to showcase the student’s abilities as a digital artist. This means that to display one’s ability to create visually and unique functional digital products that appeals to the audience point of view.

Typography

Brevia Regular & Bold. I used Brevia Regular for the contents and description of my pages, while Brevia Bold for the headings and sub-headings. I chose to use Brevia as it provides clear and simple letters making it easy to read on digital screens. It also has a modern and minimalist feel which adds to the design and having the use of Brevia Regular & Bold provides a clear hierarchy as it helps to organise the information on a page to guide my reader's eyes through the difference level of importance. I also used another font of Puffin Arcade Nerf Regular to give a playfulness look to the portfolio.
